Vbiz HR App has been developed by Vasista Enterprise Solutions (P) Ltd, a cloud based ERP Solutions provider. The HR mobile app provides all relevant employee HR information such as Profile view, leave, attendance and payslips etc that can be accessed by employees from their fingertips.

The mobile app has been highly appreciated by users for its ability to provide relevant crucial information and also for its ease of use.

Apart from this, the App in due course will also provide important information to NHDC on the Commercial module relating to Suppliers and Weaver Societies.
