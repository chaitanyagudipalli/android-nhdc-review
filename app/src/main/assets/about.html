Milkosoft is the first cloud-based Dairy ERP product built exclusively for the Indian Dairy Industry and is developed by Vasista Enterprise Solutions Pvt Ltd. All operations from procurement, production, sales, human resources, material management and finance are seamlessly integrated into Milkosoft.

This Android app provides easy access to all the daily Marketing activities related to sales of Milk and Milk Products. This app is also available for agents who can track their purchases, payments and even place indents right from their fingertips. The HR module provides access to key HR data relevant to an employee. The inventory module provides a convenient way for users to track the latest stock position for all materials available in stores
